Upon discovering MG Fitness Gym's specialized rehabilitation programs, Mark decided to give it a shot. With the help of the gym's physiotherapists and tailored workout routines, he regained strength and mobility. Now, Mark is back to playing recreational basketball and credits MG Fitness for his successful recovery.

She emphasizes that the gym not only helped her physically but also provided emotional support during her postpartum journey.
